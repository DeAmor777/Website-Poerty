Poetry Bout You :

Ke Halaman 2

1.) Whisper of Down
In the quiet hues of early light,
The world awakens, soft and bright.
Your name linger in the breath of air,
A melody whispered, tender and rare.

2.) Stardust Hearts
Our souls entwined in cosmic dance,
A fleeting gaze, a fated chance.
Like stardust caught in twilight's hand,
Together we create a boundless land,

3.) Silent Serenade
Beneath thhe moon's ethereal glow.
Word unsaid still softly flow.
Your silence speaks in tender prose,
A garden blooms where no one knows.

4.) The Echo Of You
In the mountains, in the seas,
Your voice carries on the breeze
An echo that time cannot erase,
A timeless warmth, a fleeting grace.

5.) Celestial Threads Our fates are stitched with silver thread,
By hands unseen, where stars are wed.
Each pull, each tie, a story spins,
A univers where love begins.

6.) Petals In The Wind
Scattered roses, soft and free
Dance to whispers from the sea.
Their beauty fleetinng, yet it stays,
In hearts that keep love's golden rays.

7.) Mirage Of You In the desert of my restless mind,
You are the oasis i long to find.
A vision fleeting, yet so true,
An endless dream, a mirage of you.

8.) Lantern Glow
Your laughter lights the darkest skies,
A lantern where my solace lies.
Through shadows deep, your warmth remains,
A flame that soothes all weary pains.

9.) Winter's Breath
Through winter's chill and icy air,
Your warmth lingers, bold and rare.
In every snowflake's gentle kiss,
I fell your touch, a fleeting bliss.

10.) Veil of Dusk
As dusk unfurls her velvet shroud,
The stars appear, serene and proud.
In every twinkle, i see your face,
A gentle guide, my sacred space.
